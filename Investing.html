Best Investment Options

Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs)

Benefit: Regular, disciplined investing in mutual funds.

Risk Level: Moderate

Potential Returns: 8% - 15% annually

Mutual Funds

Benefit: Diversified portfolio managed by experts.

Risk Level: Varies (Low to High)

Potential Returns: 7% - 20% annually

Stocks

Benefit: Ownership in companies with high growth potential.

Risk Level: High

Potential Returns: 10% - 30% annually

Fixed Deposits (FDs)

Benefit: Safe, guaranteed returns.

Risk Level: Low

Potential Returns: 5% - 7% annually

Real Estate

Benefit: Tangible asset with potential for long-term appreciation.

Risk Level: Medium

Potential Returns: 8% - 12% annually

Gold

Benefit: Hedge against inflation, historically stable investment.

Risk Level: Low to Medium

Potential Returns: 6% - 10% annually

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

Benefit: Government-backed, tax-free returns.

Risk Level: Low

Potential Returns: 7% - 8% annually

National Pension Scheme (NPS)

Benefit: Retirement-focused, tax benefits under Section 80CCD.

Risk Level: Moderate

Potential Returns: 8% - 10% annually
